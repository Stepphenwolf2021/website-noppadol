The long version

All about me

Born and raised in Thailand, I took my first piano lessons at five — my gateway to the music world. Growing up I chased many hobbies and sports: tennis, soccer, swimming, chess, art, programming and guitar among them.

I began at Patai Udom Suksa School, then at 13 joined Samsenwittayalai School. After three years I moved to the College of Music, Mahidol University for the pre-college program, majoring in classical piano — the moment music became my main path. I also started singing, performed in and helped arrange a range of concerts, and competed in piano competitions, including one in Osaka, Japan.

In my final high-school year I began an intensive music-technology course and entered the undergraduate major in 2019, graduating in 2023. Four years of lessons, projects and real concerts taught me invaluable, practical knowledge — and made me the confident, ambitious musician I am today.
