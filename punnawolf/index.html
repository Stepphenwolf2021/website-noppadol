About

The
craft

One voice, from the first idea to the final master.

I work across the full chain — composing and arranging, playing keys, tracking vocals, then mixing and mastering — so every record keeps a single, coherent intent from start to finish.

Trained at the College of Music, Mahidol University, I bring studio precision and a performer's instinct to records for artists, bands and brands.
